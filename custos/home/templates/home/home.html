Why would I need this?

The outbreak of new (novel coronavirus SARS-CoV-2) virulent strains are continuously infecting the people worldwide, resulting in the pandemic known as COVID-19 Virus contact through contaminated surfaces are the main reason for the spread and transmission of human coronaviruses.

The length in the persistence of novel coronavirus SARS-CoV-2 is different for different range of nonbiocidal surface materials. The risk can be increased when individuals have continuous or close contact with these contaminated items.
